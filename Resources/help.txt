MPC2Live Help
=============

GETTING STARTED
---------------
1. On your MPC, save your project. The .xpj file will be in the project folder on your SD card or internal storage.
2. Copy the project folder to your Mac (via USB, Wi-Fi, or SD card reader).
3. Drag the .xpj file onto the MPC2Live window — or click "Browse" to locate it.
4. The converted .als file will appear in the same folder as your .xpj.
5. Open the .als in Ableton Live.

WHAT GETS CONVERTED
-------------------
• Drum tracks → Ableton Drum Rack (one rack per track)
• Pads → Drum Rack slots with Simpler instruments
• Sequences → MIDI clips in the arrangement view
• Sample references → linked to your original files
• Master bus compressor → Ableton Compressor2 on the main track

TIPS
----
• Make sure your sample files are accessible on your Mac at the same relative path.
• If samples show as missing in Ableton, use "Collect All and Save" to locate them.
• For best results, use Ableton Live 11 or later.
